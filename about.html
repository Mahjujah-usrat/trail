ABOUT US

Blood Donor Website is a non-profit organization dedicated to facilitating blood donation across communities. Our mission is to connect blood donors with those in need of blood transfusions and contribute to saving lives. Our organization was founded in 2010 and has since grown to serve numerous hospitals and blood banks across the country. We work with a network of trained volunteers and partner organizations to ensure a smooth and efficient blood donation process.
